Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

StyleNest Digital ("we", "our", or "us") operates the StyleNest Content Manager application (Pinterest App ID: 1580110). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to provide our services:

  • Pinterest Account Data: With your explicit authorization via OAuth, we access your Pinterest profile, boards, and pins data.
  • Usage Data: We collect anonymous usage statistics to improve our service.

We do NOT collect: payment information, location data, contacts, or any data not explicitly authorized by you.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide content scheduling and management features
  • To display analytics about your Pinterest content performance
  • To organize and manage your Pinterest boards
  • To improve our service based on aggregate usage patterns

4. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, trade, or share your personal data with any third parties. Your data is used exclusively to provide the StyleNest Content Manager service.

5. Data Security

  • All API tokens are encrypted at rest using AES-256 encryption
  • All data transmissions use TLS 1.2+ encryption
  • Access tokens are never logged or stored in plain text
  • We follow industry-standard security practices

6. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services. You can request deletion of all your data at any time by contacting us.

7. Your Rights

  • Revoke Pinterest access at any time through your Pinterest account settings
  • Request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of all your data
  • Opt out of analytics tracking

8. Pinterest API Compliance

We comply fully with the Pinterest Developer Guidelines and API Terms of Service. We only access data authorized by the user through the OAuth consent screen, and we never exceed the scope of our API access.
